Energy Healing 
In this practice, I’ll introduce the basics of energy healing. Traditionally, energy healing is 
taught through initiation into a particular tradition, but I find that some of the techniques 
overlap with meditation. For the sake of completeness, I include a brief introduction to 
these techniques.
